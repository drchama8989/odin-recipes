Chocolate Chip Cookies

Descriptions

This cookie is crispy on the edges chewy on the inside.
Best servced with a cold glass of milk

    Ingredients
  • unsalted butter
  • brown sugar
  • white sugar
  • egg
  • vanilla extract
  • all purpose flour
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • instant espresso coffee powder
  • semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Grease a cookie sheet
  2. Cream together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg and vanilla. Sift together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and espresso powder; stir into the creamed mixture. Finally, stir in the chocolate chips
  3. Drop dough onto the baking sheet
  4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
