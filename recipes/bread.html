Foccacia

Ingredients

  • honey
  • warm water
  • active dry yeast
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • salt
  • all purpose flour

Directions

  1. Dissolve honey in warm water and add active dry yeast. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam
  2. Stir in olive oil, salt, then add flour until dough comes together. Turn dough out on a well-floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic
  3. Place dough in a lightly oiled large bowl and turn to coat. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume
  4. Preheat the oven to 415 degrees F (215 degrees C). Oil a baking sheet
  5. Place dough onto the prepared baking sheet; flatten to evenly cover the whole sheet. Press indentations into dough spaced about 1 inch apart using fingertips. Drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle kosher salt over top. Let rise for 10 minutes.
  6. Bake for about 20 minutes until golden brown
